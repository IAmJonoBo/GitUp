# Code of conduct

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and productive environment for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be kind and professional.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Give feedback that is specific and actionable.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experience levels.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or threats.
- Personal attacks, insults, or hostility.
- Sharing private information without consent.
- Disruptive behavior that derails collaboration.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness a violation, please open a private security advisory on GitHub with details. If that is not possible, open an issue with a short summary and we will follow up.

## Enforcement

We may remove content, restrict participation, or take other actions to keep the project safe and respectful.
